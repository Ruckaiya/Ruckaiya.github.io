Welcome To WEF

WE believe literacy is a basic human right, and not a privilege. WE believe literacy is the ability to read the world (not limited reading words). WE are living in an exponential time. This means socio-economic, technological, climate, and many forces that deeply impact human well being are changing at the fastest rate in the history of human civilization. These changes are affecting the marginalized people of the world and Bangladesh is no different. WE Foundation works towards democratizing literacy for creating a more equitable and just Bangladesh.
